Golden State Warriors: The Dynasty That Changed Basketball

Few teams in sports history have altered the fabric of their sport the way the Golden State Warriors did during the 2010s. With four NBA championships in eight years (2015, 2017, 2018, 2022), the Warriors didn't just win — they revolutionized how basketball is played at every level.

The "Splash Brothers" — Stephen Curry and Klay Thompson — along with Draymond Green and Kevin Durant, created a brand of basketball that prioritized three-point shooting, ball movement, and defensive versatility. Curry's unprecedented shooting range forced defenses to completely rethink how they guard the perimeter, a shift that is still felt throughout basketball today.
